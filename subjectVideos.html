Arithmetic is most probably one of the first few subjects that you learned at school. It deals with numbers and numerical computation. It is the foundation for studying other branches of mathematics. Topics in Arithmetic include whole numbers, place values, addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, factoring, fractions, decimals, exponents, scientific notations, percents, integers, proportions and word problems.
